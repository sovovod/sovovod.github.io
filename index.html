SPS 2020 München

July 31 - August 1

Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München

The Statistical Physics Symposium will be held at the LMU Munich July 31st and August 1st, 2020, coinciding with the 60th birthday of Prof. Dr. Erwin Frey. It will bring together physicists from all parts of the world, amongst them friends, colleagues and collaborators. The program will comprise two days of talks.

Program will be uploaded on 01.03.2020
